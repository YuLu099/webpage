Preparation

Here are the list of items you need to set up your "bomb"

What is Raspberry Pi

Raspberry Pi is a series of small single-board computers developed in the United Kingdom by the Raspberry Pi Foundation. It is widely used in many areas, such as for weather monitoring, because of its low cost, modularity, and open design. It is typically used by computer and electronic hobbyists, due to its adoption of HDMI and USB devices.

Setting Up

Hardwares

You will need a power supply, MicroSD card, keyboard, monitor, mouse and a HDMI cable for initial set up.

Raspberry Pi Imager

Use Rpi Imager to install Raspberry Pi OS on your SD card. Anything that’s stored on the SD card will be overwritten during formatting.

Compose

Now get everything connected to your Raspberry Pi. It’s important to do this in the right order, so that all your components are safe.

Power

Connect it to your Raspberry Pi’s power port, after a few seconds the Raspberry Pi OS desktop will appear.

Finishing

The Welcome to Raspberry Pi application will pop up and guide you through the initial setup, click next to start.

Find help

If you’re having problems with your Raspberry Pi, you can get help and advice from Raspberry Pi forum and Raspberry Jam community.
